Frequently Asked Questions

Is bleed available for free and public use?

No. You must purchase either a monthly or onetime subscription for bleed to be invited to your server.

How can I pay for bleed?

bleed costs USD$10 per month to operate. You are also able to make a one-time payment of USD$30. These prices are final and not subject for negotiation. You can pay using the following payment methods: PayPal (friends & family), Venmo, Cashapp, Bitcoin, Ethereum. Note that refunds will never be processed.

What happens if I do not want to continue paying monthly?

If you do not wish to pay monthly anymore, feel free to leave a message to jace#0819 via Discord expressing that you would like to modify your subscription. This will prevent reoccurring messages reminding you to pay your monthly subscription fee again.

What happens if I lose bleed?

If something occurs that causes you to lose bleed, feel free to leave a message to jace#0819 via Discord expressing that you would like it back in your server. Ensure to attach proof of payment in your message. If you lose bleed more than once, your previous subscription (monthly/onetime) will be voided, thus requiring you to begin a new subscription.

What happens if I want to transfer bleed to another server?

If you want to transfer bleed to another server, feel free to leave a message to jace#0819 via Discord expressing that you would like to transfer the bot to another server. Ensure to attach proof of payment, the server ID to the server that bleed is currently in, and a server ID to the server that you want bleed to be transferred to in your message. If you want bleed to be transferred more than once, your previous subscription (monthly/onetime) will be voided, thus requiring you to begin a new subscription.

What do I do after I pay?

After purchasing bleed, invite the bot by going to https://jace.lol/invite, selecting the server you paid for from the dropdown menu, selecting Continue, and then selecting Authorize. This will allow bleed to enter your server and will grant it's corresponding bot role the Administrator permission to fully operate. If neither the Administrator nor the Manage Messages permissions are granted, then bleed will prompt you with a warning to grant one of those permissions when attempting to run a command.

How can I have faith that bleed will not nuke my server?

bleed is a verified Discord bot. If bleed was to nuke your server, then the bot and its creator jace#0819 would be banished from the Discord platform. This is not a desirable outcome.

Where can I receive help if I'm confused or have any questions?

You can receive help by joining our support Discord Server. Please do not message jace#0819 via Discord if your inquiry is not urgent or if does it not revolve around payment.

How can I obtain donor perks (ex: ,lastfm mode)?

You can obtain all current (and future) perks by making a one-time donation of at least USD$10. These donations assist us with API expenses and server hosting. Nitro boosting our support Discord server twice will also allow you to obtain all current (and future) perks for as long as you continue boosting.
