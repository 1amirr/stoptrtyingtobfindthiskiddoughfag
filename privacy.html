Why do you need the data and how is it used?

when a command is invoked, we store that message content for a maximum of 14 days for debugging purposes. we also store deleted & edited message content for a maximum of 24 hours and is usually overwritten immediately as we only store the latest entry for the "snipe" and "editsnipe" commands.

guild ids, channel ids, role ids, user ids and message ids are all stored for our system to figure out what [something here] belongs to or when our functions require that data

nickname and username changes are logged in order for the "namehistory" command to function. users can clear this data themselves at any time.

Who is your collected information shared with?

We do not sell and expose your information to others/third parties by any means.
